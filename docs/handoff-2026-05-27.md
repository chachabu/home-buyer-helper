## Current Recommendation Interpretation

Default ranking is not an investment-yield ranking. It sorts by:

- Metro/commute friendliness.
- Rental-fit layout and area.
- Liquidity.
- Condition.
- School reference.

Rent and rent yield appear in output to help review the listing, but they do not change default score unless `--weight-rent` is explicitly set.
